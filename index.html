About Me

Hello, my name is Saitama, also known as One Punch Man.

I’m from Z-City, Japan and my birthday is April 21, 1990.

I trained so hard that I lost all my hair, and now I’m a professional hero ranked in the Hero Association.

My mentor was Bang (Silver Fang), and my student is Genos.

My hobbies include grocery shopping on sale days, playing video games, and sometimes oversleeping.

My dream? Honestly, I just wanted to be a hero for fun… but now I seek a real challenge.

“Human beings are strong because we can change ourselves.” – Saitama
